FAQ

Common Questions:

Q:   When will I be paid once I sell an item with Rekloth?

A:   We begin processing payments on the 15th of every month for the previous month's sales. For example, any sales made in March will be paid between the 15th and 30th of April, and any sales made in April will be paid between the 15th and 31st of May. You will be paid based on what you chose at the beginning of each consignment (cash through PayPal or Rekloth Store Credit).


Q:   Are "new" items actually new?

A:   Sort of. All of our new items are consigned with us and still have the original store tag attached. We inspect all new and used items exactly the same way to ensure quality and authenticity.


Q:   I want an item I sent to Rekloth back that is currently listed on the site, how to I get it before it sells?

A:   Email us at info@rekloth.com and we will resolve the issue. If the request is made before the item has been listed for a total of 90 days, shipping fees may apply. If you make the request after a total of 90 days of the item being listed, we will return the item to you for free.


Q:   Help! I accidentally sent an item to Rekloth by mistake! I feel like it is going to be accepted. Can I get it back before it is listed or donated?

A:   As soon as you realize you want something back, please email us at info@rekloth.com so we can do our best to return your item(s). To ensure that we don't donate anything that was sent by mistake. We wait to donate any unaccepted items until we have listed everything on Rekloth that was accepted. We list items on Rekloth between one day and four weeks after delivery of your consignment.
